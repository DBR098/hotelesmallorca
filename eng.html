Sant Francesc Hotel Singular

Sant Francesc Hotel Singular Palma is a 5 star boutique hotel, located in a Neoclassical manor house from the 19th century, in the heart of the old town of Palma de Mallorca. It counts with 42 rooms and suites. Inside, a warm and sophisticated decoration is discovered. The location of the hotel is ideal to enjoy the city on foot, as well as the rest of the island of Mallorca.

Boutique Hotel Can Alomar

The 5-star Boutique Hotel Can Alomar is located in the building of the same name. An old urban manor house, cataloged and protected, of historical, artistic and architectural interest and great heritage value. The hotel has 16 spacious and bright rooms decorated in a modern design. a gastronomic restaurant serving Mediterranean, Japanese, Peruvian fusion and a terrace solarium for the exclusive use of guests of the hotel.

Hipotels Playa de Palma Palace

The Hipotels Playa de Palma Palace is a spectacular 5 star hotel located on Playa de Palma, Mallorca. A newly built hotel ideal for holidays or business, with a luxurious spa and special prices for playing golf. A modern and elegant interior with spacious design rooms, which will offer you some perfect vacation with all the complementary leisure offer of Playa de Palma. You can't miss visiting the chill out area on the top floor of the hotel, our Heaven Rooftop Bar with unique views of Playa de Palma and a sunset that, accompanied by a refreshing cocktail and music in direct, will become one of the unforgettable moments of your vacation.
